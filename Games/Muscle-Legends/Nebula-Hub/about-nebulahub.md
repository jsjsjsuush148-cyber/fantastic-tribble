## 📜 The Story

**Nebula Hub** wasn't just another executor hub. It was *the* hub – for a while.

Originally, it supported only two games:

- 🏃 **Legends of Speed**
- 💪 **Muscle Legends**

That's it. Two games. And yet… it exploded.

Why? Because it *worked*. Smooth UI. Reliable scripts. No bullshit backdoors (at first). The kind of hub you'd leave running overnight and wake up to maxed stats.

No warning.

No goodbye.
